## 📱 Quick Actions Guide

### As a Visitor:
1. Click **"View All"** to see all properties
2. Use **filters** on the properties page
3. Click **"Login"** to access more features
4. Click **"Register"** to create an account

### As a Buyer:
1. Click **"Dashboard"** after login
2. Browse properties via navigation
3. Click **"Book Appointment"** on any property
4. Check **"My Appointments"** in dashboard
5. **Make Payment** when booking is accepted
6. **Give Feedback** after completion

### As a Seller:
1. Click **"Dashboard"** after login
2. Click **"Add New Property"** button
3. Fill property details and submit
4. Check **"Booking Requests"** sidebar
5. **Accept** or **Reject** appointments
6. View **Payments Received**

### As an Admin:
1. Click **"Dashboard"** after login
2. Use **sidebar** to access features
3. **Manage Users** - Activate/Deactivate
4. **Verify Documents** - Approve/Reject
5. **Generate Invoice** for bookings
6. **View Analytics** on dashboard

## 🐛 Common Questions

**Q: Can I register a new account?**
A: Yes! Click "Register" and create a Buyer or Seller account.

**Q: What happens after I book an appointment?**
A: The seller receives a notification and can accept or reject your request.

**Q: How do I make a payment?**
A: Once seller accepts your booking, you'll see a "Make Payment" button in "My Appointments".

**Q: Can I add real properties?**
A: Yes! Login as seller and use "Add New Property" feature.

**Q: How do admins generate invoices?**
A: Navigate to "Generate Invoice" in admin dashboard, select a booking, and fill details.

**Q: Can I edit my profile?**
A: Yes! Click on your user icon → Profile, then update your information.
